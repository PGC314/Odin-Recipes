Sweet and Sour Chicken

An extremely tasty alternative to plain chicken breast.

This recipe is great for when you want to get a few servings of high protein meals with quite a lot flavor.

Works great for when you can't be bothered to make rice or pasta with good seasoning or sauce!

Ingredients:

For the sauce:

  • (1/2 cup)125ml soy sauce
  • (1/3 cup)80ml store bought pineapple juice
  • (4 Tbsp)80grams honey
  • (2 Tsbp)40ml worcestershire sauce
  • (2 Tbsp)40ml apple cider vinegar or white vinegar
  • (2 Tbsp)40grams tomato sauce/ketchup
  • (2 Tbsp)12grams cornflour or corn starch

The rest of the dish:

  • (2.5 Tbsp)50ml oil
  • 1.4kg chicken breast diced
  • 3 large bell peppers diced
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 4 garlic cloves minced
  • 15gram ginger minced

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together the sauce ingredients in a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Pour bit of oil into a large pan or pot over high heat and add the vegetables.
  3. Sprinke with salt and saute the vegetables for around 4 minutes then remove the vegetables and set aside in a bowl.
  4. Add a bit more oil to the pan and toss in the diced chicken breast.
  5. Salt the chicken lightly and sear for around 6 minutes, mix the chicken around a bit during this time.
  6. Add the minced garlic & ginger to the chicken and mix for around a minute.
  7. Add the sauted veggies back and mix again for about another minute.
  8. Whisk the sauce and add it to the pan.
  9. Bring the sauce to a boil and cook for another 2 minutes while mixing to get everything well coated.
  10. Serve with your preferred choice of carbs and enjoy!
